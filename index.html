About 🥳

Hello! I'm U.P. Nguyen (she/her/hers). I'm a software engineer, outdoor educator, and run coach currently based in Cambridge, MA and working on Brooks Running’s BAM (Brooks Analytics Machine) team.

I was born in Albuquerque, NM, and lived there until I moved to Providence, RI to attend Brown University. There, I earned a degree in computer science in 2019 and took classes across 13 different disciplines including environmental sciences, economics, and performing arts.

As a software engineer, I've been an intern @ Automattic (Wordpress's parent company) where I worked on security and performance tools for VIP clients such as San Jose Mercury News and Facebook. I also worked as a communications and hardware intern for the Brown University Information Technology department, and updated the wireless services offered to the university's 8,000 member student population. 👩‍🎓

When I'm not at a screen, you can find me in the woods and in the mountains. I believe strongly in the transformational power of the outdoors and of intentional experiences, and have over a decade of experience leading and facilitating backpacking and rock climbing trips for middle and high schoolers, college students, and adults. I most recently finished a season of guiding and instructing at North Carolina Outward Bound School (NCOBS) 🏔

I'm a proudly queer 🏳️‍🌈, first-generation American and first-generation college graduate. I am passionate about diversity, equity, and inclusion, and across all facets of my life I advocate for marginalized groups and work to break down barriers. Ask me about my life story and how I came to be in the United States!

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, check out my projects on Github, or stalk me on Twitter. You can also email me.
